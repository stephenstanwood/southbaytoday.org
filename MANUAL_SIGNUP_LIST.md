## Tips

- **CivicPlus cities** (Saratoga, Campbell, Los Altos, Los Gatos, Gilroy, Morgan Hill, Monte Sereno, Milpitas) now require creating a free account before you can subscribe. Create one CivicPlus account per site — painful but one-time.
- **Chambers** mostly use ChamberMaster / GrowthZone — find the 'Newsletter Signup' link in their footer or 'About' page.
- **WAF-blocked big cities** (Palo Alto, Mountain View, Sunnyvale, Santa Clara, Cupertino, San Jose) have bot-protected signup pages. Signing up via a real browser should work fine.
- **Newspapers** (Mercury News, Palo Alto Online) usually have a newsletter signup page with 10+ options — pick the 'events' / 'things to do' / 'daily headlines' list.
- **Skip anything that requires a full account with password** (unless you actually want an account). The tracker's `status: needs-manual` just means I couldn't auto-subscribe — YOU can still decide to pass.
